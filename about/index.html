About Me

Hey there! My name is Natalie.

I’m a blockchain developer, and I love it. I’m also a blogger - and at times, have a passion for writing (other times, I’ve just got a brick wall that I can’t get past).

I was first introduced to Blockchain at ETHWaterloo, where I built a P2P System where I learnt a whole lot about the blockchain space. I became passionate about learning more and understanding more about the technology.

As my first blockchain developer position, I worked at STK, where I worked on a payment channel that would allow users to spend cryptocurrency at point of sale. We first built it to be multi-token - thus supporting any token that met ERC20 standards. Since then, we’ve also been able to convert ETH to WETH behind the scenes, so a user doesn’t have to understand the difference. The code has since launched on mainnet and the project is in its beta stages.

I am currently working as a Full Stack Developer at Intelliware Inc, a client consulting firm in Toronto. I am on a React Project, and excited to learn the skills required to build the front-end of applications.

In my spare time, I teach at George Brown College in Toronto, in the first blockchain program in the world. It’s been a wonderful and eye-opening experience to inspire and teach the next round of blockchain developers. I am also an editor of Mastering Solidity and am excited to see the future launch of the book.

I am passionate about sharing my knowledge of blockchain with others. I have done many Introduction to Solidity talks to participants at hackathons. I am also passionate about diversity and women in tech, and particularly, women in blockchain.

To reach me, feel free to click on any of the social media links on the page. Cheers!
